# CENTRAL TEACHING PROGRESSION

**Adam & Eve:** Kindness starts with **trusting God** and **choosing right over selfish desires**

**Noah:** Kindness means **persevering** and **protecting** even when others mock you

**Moses:** Kindness is **standing up** for the oppressed and being **patient** with those who resist

**Jesus:** Kindness is **resisting temptation** and staying true to God even when **vulnerable and alone**

All four stories answer the same question: **Is it better to be kind to others and obey God, or is it better to be selfish?**
